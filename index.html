TABLETOP GAMING IN THE LUNE VALLEY

Lune Valley Tabletop Alliance is a new tabletop gaming club in the Lune Valley, aimed at forming a community for people to find people who love the games they do, sharing them and bringing others into it. We will be hosting a club night once a week in Hornby.

BOOK TABLES FOR YOUR FAVOURITE GAMES

Club members are able to book a table in advance to ensure they get their spot on a club night. Guests are able to book a table 24 hours before.

Players can take advantage of the using the clubs wargaming boards and scenery as the club grows its collection. We also have ambitions of investing in our own 3D printer to start printing all types of terrain and tabletop gadgets!

GOT AN IDEA FOR SCENERY? LETS 3D PRINT IT…

One of the big goals for the club is to invest in a 3D printer as soon as we can. Nobody likes a flat table for wargaming… We want to encourage our members to come up with ideas for interesting scenery, tools and accessories and we’ll work together to make it come to life!

These ambitions can only be realised through people joining the club so check out our memberships here.

A PLACE FOR EVERYONE TO PLAY ANY GAME

One of our core values is to be a friendly and welcoming place for people to come and play the games they love and find new games. We encourage people to play new games and play with new people. We do not tolerate abusive, anti-social or elitist behaviour.

The club is a safe space for people to play the games they love.
