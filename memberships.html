CLUB MEMBERSHIPS

BECOME A 2024 FOUNDING MEMBER OF THE CLUB

Members who join the club in the 2024 calendar year, will earn a founding membership. For as long as you remain a member, your annual club t-shirt/hoodie/ lanyard will include a founder status upon it.

Register

BOOK TABLES FOR YOUR FAVOURITE GAMES

Club members are able to book a table in advance to ensure they get their spot on a club night. Guests are able to book a table 24 hours before.

Players can take advantage of the using the clubs wargaming boards and scenery as the club grows its collection. We also have ambitions of investing in our own 3D printer to start printing all types of terrain and tabletop gadgets!

COMPETE WITH OTHERS AT CLUB EVENTS

With enough members, we want to start hosting tournaments for the clubs favourite games. Play against others to win prizes and climb the club leaderboards.

Tournament days can be hosted by the club for up to 150 people, providing a fully licensed bar, catering facilities and limited free parking.
